A note from the founder
The story behind Flipzy

When I was a kid, my favorite thing in the world was Disney read-along tapes. You would pop in the cassette, open the book, and when you heard the chime — you knew it was time to turn the page. It was pure magic. The story came alive with voices, music, and sound effects, but the book was always in your hands.

I have been chasing that feeling ever since. And when I became a parent, I wanted my kids to have that same experience — but those tapes are long gone. So I started building Flipzy: a way to bring that read-along magic back, for any book, without handing your child a screen.

Flipzy is not about replacing the parent. It is about making the reading moment richer, more playful, and something kids ask for again and again. If you remember that chime, you already understand what we are building.
