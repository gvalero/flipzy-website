How StoryGlow works

StoryGlow is built to be simple. No complicated setup. No distracting interface. Just a better way to enjoy books together.

1

Pick a book

Open your child’s favorite story and get ready for bedtime as normal.

2

Start StoryGlow

StoryGlow begins reading with warm narration, character voices, and sound effects.

3

Enjoy the moment together

Your child stays engaged, and you stay part of the story instead of being pushed out by technology.

Does StoryGlow replace the parent?

No. StoryGlow is built to support storytime, not take it over. The goal is a shared moment, not a substitute.

Does this use screens?

No. StoryGlow is designed around keeping the magic in books, imagination, and togetherness.

Who is it best for?

Families with young children, especially those around ages 3–6, who want bedtime to feel easier and more engaging.
